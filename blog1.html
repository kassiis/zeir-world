Dossier — النظام و المدارس العظيمة

A compact, navigable reference of the kingdom: governance, noble ranks, administrative divisions, and the schools that shaped its thought — crafted to be used by writers, artists, and game designers.

Author: Youssef
Setting: High-mana feudal realm
Format: Modular • Printable
Quick summary
A Sultanate with semi-feudal dukes controlling great domains, a central Diwan (Senate), and a layered administration down to municipal supervisors. Schools replace religion as institutions of thought.
Why it exists
To let creators explore political dynamics, slow-changing cultures due to long lifespans, and the interplay between arcane power (الزير) and governance.
Usage
  • Reference for dialogue, protocols, and ceremonies.
  • Framework for factional conflicts and plots.
  • Source material for art assets and tabletop campaigns.

Hierarchy & Administrative Map

Clear chain of command and noble ranks.

السلطان — Sultan• Supreme ruler, issues decrees
الديوان — Diwan (Senate)• Council of dukes & advisors
الداي — Da'i (Minister)• Chief administrator
الآغا — Agha (General)• Military commander
بايلم — Baylim (Domain steward)• Oversees an iqta' / fief
زيخ — Zikh (Governor/Chief)• Province / tribal leader
مشرف — Supervisor• Town / municipal manager
Nobility tiers

دوق (Duke) — regional lord; دون (Don) — provincial noble; فون (Von) — city noble, distant from court.

Administrative notes

Some provinces are managed directly by the central state (the capital, mana-rich regions). Local stewards retain traditional rights but owe tribute & men-at-arms.

The Great Schools (المدارس العظيمة)

Cultural institutions that function like religions, universities, and martial orders combined.

Types

فنية — magical / mana cultivation. قتالية — martial mastery. مختلطة — unified schools that combine both.

Societal role

They educate state officials, grant prestige, and function as political actors. Each school has archives, training grounds, and often a patron noble house.

Example entries
School of the Quiet Blade — Fighting school focused on slow, precise techniques and conditioning.
River of Golden Thought — A mana school famous for agricultural & artisan spells.
